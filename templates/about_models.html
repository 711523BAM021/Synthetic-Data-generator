How Our AI Works

Gaussian Copula Model

Used for structured tabular data. The model learns statistical correlations between columns to generate realistic new rows that preserve the original distribution.

LSTM Time-Series Model

Automatically detected when date/time columns are present. Uses Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural networks to learn temporal sequences and patterns.

Limitations

  • Very small datasets (under 30 rows) may reduce realism.
  • Time-series generation requires numeric signals.
  • Pure ID datasets (unique identifiers only) cannot be effectively modeled.
